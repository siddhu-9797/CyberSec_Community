Ransomware Crisis: Objectives

  • Identify initial infection vector.
  • Contain spread across network segments.
  • Analyze ransomware variant & capabilities.
  • Evaluate backup integrity and recovery options.
  • Simulate communication with stakeholders/executives.
  • (Optional) Engage in mock negotiation tactics.
  • Execute recovery plan and verify system integrity.

DDoS Overload: Objectives

  • Detect and classify DDoS attack type (volumetric, application-layer, etc.).
  • Implement appropriate mitigation strategies (rate limiting, IP filtering, CDN/WAF config).
  • Analyze traffic patterns to identify source IPs/networks or botnets.
  • Monitor critical service availability and performance impacts.
  • Communicate status updates to relevant internal teams.
  • Adjust mitigation as attack evolves.

Critical Data Breach: Objectives

  • Confirm breach occurrence and scope the extent of data exposure.
  • Identify compromised systems and attacker's point of entry/persistence.
  • Contain the breach to prevent further data loss.
  • Preserve evidence for forensic analysis.
  • Assess regulatory and legal notification requirements (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).
  • Develop internal and external communication plans.
  • Coordinate with legal, PR, and executive teams.
  • Eradicate attacker presence and restore affected systems securely.

Insider Threat: Objectives

  • Investigate suspicious activity flagged by monitoring tools (UEBA, DLP).
  • Correlate logs and access patterns to identify potential insider.
  • Discreetly gather evidence while minimizing operational disruption.
  • Assess the scope and intent of the insider's actions (malicious vs. accidental).
  • Coordinate with HR, Legal, and Management on appropriate response.
  • Revoke access and secure affected data/systems.
  • Conduct post-incident review to improve internal controls.
